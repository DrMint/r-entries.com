## Ingredients

- 250&nbsp;g of water
- 100&nbsp;g of salted butter
- 150&nbsp;g of flour
- 1/2 sachet of baking powder (~5&nbsp;g)
- 1 tablespoon of sugar
- 3 whole eggs
- Pearl sugar for the topping

## Steps

1. Preheat your convection (aka fan-assisted) oven to 170&nbsp;°C.
2. In a pan, bring the water (250&nbsp;g) and the butter (100&nbsp;g) to a boil.
3. As soon as it does, remove from the heat and add the flour (150&nbsp;g), and baking powder (1/2 sachet).
4. Mix until the dough forms a ball. Let it cool down for a couple minutes.
5. Add the sugar (1 tablespoon) and the eggs (3) one after the other, mixing well after each addition.
6. Pour the mixture into a piping bag and pipe small balls onto a baking sheet. Make sure to leave plenty of space between them.
7. Sprinkle with pearl sugar. Press them gently to stick.
8. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.
9. Let them cool down for a couple minutes.
