## Ingredients

- 400g flour
- 330ml milk
- 100g butter
- 75g sugar
- 2 whole eggs
- 1 sachet of baking powder
- A pinch of salt

## Steps

1. In a big bowl, pour the flour (400g), baking powder (1 sachet), pinch of salt, and sugar (75g). Mix together and make a well in the center.
2. Beat the 2 whole eggs in another bowl.
3. Melt the butter (100g) in the microwave and pour it in the well, along with the eggs.
4. Slowly pour the milk (330ml) while mixing until homogenized.
5. That's it, you can now cook the waffles!
