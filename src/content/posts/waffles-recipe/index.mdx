This is my grandmother's waffles: gotta respect the old-timey recipes!

## Ingredients

<MdxImage src={image2} />

- 400&nbsp;g flour
- 330&nbsp;ml milk
- 100&nbsp;g butter
- 75&nbsp;g sugar
- 2 whole eggs
- 1 sachet of baking powder
- A pinch of salt

## Steps

1. In a big bowl, pour the flour (400&nbsp;g), baking powder (1 sachet), pinch of salt, and sugar (75&nbsp;g). Mix together and make a well in the center.
2. Beat the 2 whole eggs in another bowl.
3. Melt the butter (100&nbsp;g) in the microwave and pour it in the well, along with the eggs.
4. Slowly pour the milk (330&nbsp;ml) while mixing until homogenized.
5. That's it, you can now cook the waffles!
