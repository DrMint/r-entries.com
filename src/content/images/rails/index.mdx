> I wake up with difficulties. I try to look around me, but it's all black.
> I remain in this position for a moment. That's when I notice a cold thing against my neck.
> I start to panic. I understand that I have a bag on my head and I got in trouble, big trouble.
> I do my best to concentrate. I truggle and try to remove the bag but my arms are attached.
> I try to get up and fall: I can't breathe, can't BREATHE...
> 
> I don't know how long I'm here, trying to get rid of this knot...
> Did I fall asllep in the meantime? I don't even know.
> I try to call for help but nothing comes out.
> 
> Suddently, I see a light through the mesh of the bag.
> Please help me, I don't know why I'm here, I need help...

This was an English class assignment, I had to write the back cover of a book and try to make it engaging and interesting.
